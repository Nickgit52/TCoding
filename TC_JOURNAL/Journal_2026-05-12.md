- **Smart-rebuild logic is per-contract, not whole-file.** `needs_rebuild()` compares the last 2 contracts' tick counts against `{symbol}_meta.json`. Historical contracts are never re-read in smart mode — they live inside the parquet and are preserved across rebuilds.
